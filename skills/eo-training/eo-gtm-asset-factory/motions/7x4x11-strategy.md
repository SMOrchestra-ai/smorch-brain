# 7x4x11 Strategy — Motion Assets

**Best for:** Founders who want to dominate through consistent, multi-platform content distribution.
**MENA Note:** Content distribution in MENA is less saturated than Western markets. Consistency wins faster.

---

## Assets Produced

### 1. Content Distribution Matrix (DOCX)
**File:** `7x4x11-strategy/content-distribution-matrix.docx`

The 7x4x11 framework: 7 platforms, 4 content formats, 11 weekly touchpoints.

```
YOUR 7x4x11 MATRIX:

PLATFORMS (pick your 7 from ICP access channels):
1. LinkedIn (mandatory for B2B)
2. YouTube
3. Twitter/X
4. Email newsletter
5. WhatsApp (MENA essential)
6. [Community platform — Slack/Discord/Facebook Group]
7. [Industry-specific — Product Hunt, Reddit, Indie Hackers, etc.]

FORMATS (4 types, rotate weekly):
A. Text (LinkedIn posts, tweets, newsletter)
B. Video (YouTube, LinkedIn video, short-form)
C. Visual (carousels, infographics, screenshots)
D. Audio (voice notes, podcast clips, Spaces)

WEEKLY CADENCE (11 touchpoints):
Mon: LinkedIn text post (insight from your space)
Tue: Twitter thread OR YouTube video publish
Wed: Newsletter issue + WhatsApp community update
Thu: LinkedIn carousel or visual content
Fri: Community engagement (respond, comment, share)
Sat: LinkedIn text post (weekend story/reflection)
Sun: Content planning for next week

+ 4 daily micro-actions:
- Comment on 2 Dream 100 posts
- Reply to DMs/comments
- Share 1 piece of curated content
- Engage in 1 community thread
```

### 2. Platform Format Guide (DOCX)
**File:** `7x4x11-strategy/platform-format-guide.docx`

Specs and best practices per platform.

```
LINKEDIN:
- Post length: 150-300 words (sweet spot)
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM local time
- Format: Hook line → story/insight → takeaway → CTA
- Hashtags: 3 max, relevant not generic
- Carousel: 8-12 slides, 1 idea per slide, big text
- Video: Under 3 min, captions mandatory, native upload

YOUTUBE:
- Title: [Number] + [Outcome] + [Timeframe] format works best
- Thumbnail: Face + text + contrast colors
- Length: 8-15 min (sweet spot for algorithm)
- First 30 sec: hook with the outcome/result
- CTA at 60% mark (not just the end)

TWITTER/X:
- Thread: 5-10 tweets, each standalone valuable
- Hook tweet: contrarian take or specific number
- Quote tweet Dream 100 with added insight
- Spaces: host weekly on your niche topic

EMAIL NEWSLETTER:
- Subject: Under 50 chars, curiosity-driven
- Length: 500-800 words
- Structure: 1 insight + 1 resource + 1 CTA
- Send: Same day/time every week (habit)

WHATSAPP:
- Broadcast lists (not groups) for announcements
- Community channels for ongoing engagement
- Voice notes for personal touches
- Max 2 broadcasts per week (don't spam)

COMMUNITY:
- Lead with questions, not statements
- Share wins and losses (vulnerability builds trust)
- Help others before promoting yourself
- Pin valuable resources and templates
```

### 3. 90-Day Content Roadmap (DOCX)
**File:** `7x4x11-strategy/90-day-content-roadmap.docx`

Month-by-month content plan aligned to the founder's positioning.

```
MONTH 1: ESTABLISH AUTHORITY
Theme: "I understand your problem better than anyone"
- 4 LinkedIn posts on ICP pains (1/week)
- 2 YouTube videos: Tutorial + Contrarian take
- 4 newsletter issues: Teaching frameworks
- Daily: Comment, engage, build relationships
- Goal: 500 new followers / subscribers combined

MONTH 2: BUILD PROOF
Theme: "Here's evidence this works"
- 4 LinkedIn posts mixing stories + case studies
- 2 YouTube videos: Case study + Demo
- 4 newsletter issues: Results, behind-the-scenes, resources
- Daily: Same engagement cadence
- Goal: 1000 total followers, 50 conversations started

MONTH 3: CONVERT
Theme: "Here's how to get these results"
- 4 LinkedIn posts with product mentions (subtle, value-first)
- 2 YouTube videos: Full tutorial using your product + before/after
- 4 newsletter issues: Value + offer integration
- Launch webinar or workshop
- Goal: 25 product signups / demos booked
```

## MENA Adaptations

- Arabic content on LinkedIn has 2-3x less competition than English in MENA
- WhatsApp broadcast + voice notes is the #1 underused distribution channel for B2B in Gulf
- YouTube Arabic tech/business content is massively underserved
- Ramadan is prime content consumption time (people scroll more during rest hours)
