# Paid VSL Value Ladder — Motion Assets

**Best for:** Founders with budget for paid ads who want to use video sales letters to convert cold traffic.
**MENA Note:** Paid acquisition in MENA is cheaper per click than US/EU. Arabic VSLs have very low competition.

---

## Assets Produced

### 1. VSL Script (DOCX)
**File:** `paid-vsl/vsl-script.docx`

Video Sales Letter script (3-5 minutes) optimized for cold traffic.

```
SECTION 1: HOOK (0:00-0:15)
"If you're a [ICP] struggling with [pain], watch the next 3 minutes.
I'm going to show you [specific outcome] without [common painful method]."

SECTION 2: PROBLEM AGITATION (0:15-0:45)
"Here's what's happening right now:
- [Pain 1 — specific, measurable]
- [Pain 2 — emotional impact]
- [Pain 3 — cost of inaction]
And the solutions out there? [Why existing solutions fail]"

SECTION 3: SOLUTION INTRODUCTION (0:45-1:30)
"I'm [Name]. After [credibility context], I built [Venture Name].
It [what it does] by [unique mechanism].
Here's what that looks like:"
[Demo/walkthrough — show the outcome]

SECTION 4: PROOF (1:30-2:30)
"[User 1] used this to [result]."
"[User 2] went from [before] to [after] in [time]."
"[Data point or third-party validation]"

SECTION 5: OFFER (2:30-3:30)
"Here's what you get:
- [Feature/benefit 1]
- [Feature/benefit 2]
- [Feature/benefit 3]
- [Bonus if applicable]
All for [price / free trial]."

SECTION 6: CTA (3:30-4:00)
"Click below to [action]. Takes 30 seconds.
If it's not for you, no worries. But if [pain] is costing you [cost],
this is the fix. [Repeat CTA]."
```

### 2. Ad Copy Variants (DOCX)
**File:** `paid-vsl/ad-copy-variants.docx`

3 ad copy sets for LinkedIn Ads, Google Ads, and Meta Ads.

```
LINKEDIN ADS (3 variants):
Ad 1 (Pain-led): "Still [pain activity]? [Venture Name] [outcome] automatically."
Ad 2 (Proof-led): "[X] [ICP] already [outcome]. See how → [link]"
Ad 3 (Question-led): "What if you could [outcome] without [pain method]?"

GOOGLE ADS (3 variants):
Headline 1: "[Outcome] for [ICP] | Try Free"
Headline 2: "Stop [Pain] — [Venture Name]"
Headline 3: "[Competitor] Alternative | [Key Differentiator]"
Description: "[One sentence value prop]. Start free today."

META ADS (3 variants):
Primary text: "[Hook question]. [1 sentence about the product]. [Social proof]. [CTA]."
Headline: "[Outcome] in [Timeframe]"
Description: "Free trial — no credit card."
```

### 3. Landing Page Conversion Flow (DOCX)
**File:** `paid-vsl/conversion-flow.docx`

```
AD → VSL LANDING PAGE → SIGNUP/TRIAL

VSL LANDING PAGE STRUCTURE:
1. Video embed (autoplay, muted, with captions)
2. Headline below video: "[Outcome] for [ICP]"
3. 3 benefit bullets
4. CTA button (same as video CTA)
5. Testimonial strip
6. FAQ (3 questions addressing top objections)
7. Final CTA with urgency element

RETARGETING SEQUENCE:
- Watched 25%: Ad showing extended demo
- Watched 50%: Ad with testimonial focus
- Watched 75%: Ad with offer/pricing focus
- Visited pricing: Ad with trial/discount CTA
```

## MENA Adaptations

- LinkedIn Ads CPM in MENA is 40-60% cheaper than US — great ROI for B2B
- Arabic video ads get significantly higher view-through rates than English for local audience
- Google Ads for Arabic BOFU keywords have very low competition and CPC
- Meta Ads work well for awareness but LinkedIn converts better for B2B in Gulf
