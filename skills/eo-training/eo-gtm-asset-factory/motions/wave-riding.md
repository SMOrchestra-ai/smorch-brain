# Wave Riding Distribution — Motion Assets

**Best for:** Founders who can ship fast and capitalize on trends, news, and cultural moments.
**MENA Note:** MENA has specific waves — Ramadan, national days, policy announcements, tech events.

---

## Assets Produced

### 1. Wave Detection Framework (DOCX)
**File:** `wave-riding/wave-detection-framework.docx`

```
WAVE TYPES TO MONITOR:

1. INDUSTRY WAVES (recurring)
   - Funding announcements in your vertical
   - Competitor launches or failures
   - Market reports and research releases
   - Regulation changes affecting your ICP

2. CULTURAL WAVES (predictable)
   - Ramadan (content consumption spike, gifting, community)
   - National days (UAE National Day, Saudi National Day, etc.)
   - Back to business (Sep-Oct, Jan-Feb)
   - End of year planning (Nov-Dec)
   - Major tech conferences (LEAP, GITEX, Step, etc.)

3. TREND WAVES (unpredictable)
   - Viral content in your niche
   - New technology launch (AI models, platforms, tools)
   - Celebrity/influencer mentions of your category
   - Policy announcements (Saudi Vision 2030, UAE digital economy)

4. PLATFORM WAVES (algorithm-driven)
   - New platform features (LinkedIn articles, YouTube Shorts, etc.)
   - Algorithm changes favoring certain content types
   - Platform-specific events (Product Hunt daily, Twitter Spaces trending)

MONITORING TOOLS:
- Google Alerts: [your industry] + [your region]
- LinkedIn: Follow key hashtags and influencers
- Twitter Lists: Industry leaders, journalists, competitors
- Feedly/RSS: Industry publications
```

## MENA Adaptations

- Ramadan is the #1 wave riding opportunity for Arabic content (highest social media usage)
- Government policy announcements (Vision 2030, Digital Economy) create instant content opportunities
- GITEX and LEAP are the two biggest tech events — plan content around both
- National pride moments (achievements, milestones) drive massive engagement
