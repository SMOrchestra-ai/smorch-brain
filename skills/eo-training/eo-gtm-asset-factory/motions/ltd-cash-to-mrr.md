# LTD Cash-to-MRR Ladder — Motion Assets

**Best for:** Early-stage products that need cash injection and early users. Sell lifetime deals, then transition to recurring.
**MENA Note:** LTD culture exists but is price-sensitive. AppSumo doesn't serve MENA well — opportunity for localized approach.

---

```

PRICING TIERS:
Tier 1 (Early Bird — first 50): $[X] lifetime

[All tiers include: what's included, usage limits, future MRR price comparison]

- Own platform (preferred) — full control, full margin

- WhatsApp/Telegram groups for MENA founders
```

### 3. LTD-to-MRR Transition Plan (DOCX)
**File:** `ltd-cash-to-mrr/ltd-to-mrr-transition.docx`

```
THE TRANSITION (after LTD period ends):

MONTH 1 POST-LTD:
- Close all LTD tiers permanently
- Announce monthly/annual pricing
- LTD users keep their access (honor the deal — your reputation depends on it)
- New users see regular pricing only

MONTH 2-3:
- Feature gate: new premium features available only on paid plans
- LTD users get core features forever, premium features for 12 months
- After 12 months: LTD users can upgrade to paid for premium, keep core forever

MESSAGING TO LTD USERS:
"You got in early. Your core access is forever. We've added [premium features]
available on our [plan name]. As a founding user, here's your exclusive upgrade: [offer]."

METRICS TO TRACK:
- LTD revenue collected: $[target]
- LTD users converted to paid: [target %]
- Churn rate of LTD users at 12-month mark
- MRR from non-LTD users after transition
```

## MENA Adaptations

- LTD pricing should account for MENA purchasing power — $49-99 LTD sweet spot
- Arabic LTD communities are small but growing — Telegram groups are the hub
- Bank transfer / local payment options important for Egyptian and Jordanian buyers
- Trust signal: "Based in [MENA city]" or "Built for MENA" increases conversion
