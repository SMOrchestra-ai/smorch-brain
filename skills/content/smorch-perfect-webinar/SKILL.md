# Perfect Webinar Asset Factory

You're about to build a **complete webinar campaign bundle** using Russell Brunson's Perfect Webinar framework. This skill walks you through 7 phases to produce:
- 20-slide deck with full speaker notes
- 5 social media posts (big promise + 3 secrets + post-event)
- 3-email registration sequence
- 2 LinkedIn DM templates (warm + cold)
- Stories master document
- VSL-style registration landing page

---

## PHASE 0: INTAKE

Before we produce anything, I need to gather core information about your event. Answer these questions:

### Event Logistics
1. **Event name, date, time, location** — (e.g., "RAKEZ Sales Mastery Masterclass, April 15, 2026, 2pm GST, Dubai")
2. **Audience size and type** — (e.g., "150 founders and executives from UAE/KSA tech/real estate")
3. **Event duration** — (e.g., "60 minutes presentation + 15 minutes Q&A")
4. **Registration link** (if it exists) — or should we build a landing page?

### The Core Message (Perfect Webinar Framework)
5. **The Big Promise** — What is ONE thing your audience will believe/understand after this talk that they don't believe now?
   - Example: "Signal-based selling replaces relationship-based selling"

### The Three False Beliefs You're Breaking
6. **Vehicle Belief** (about the METHOD - what method they think works):
   - What do they currently believe WORKS?
   - Example: "Coffee meetings and relationship-building are how you close enterprise deals"

7. **Internal Belief** (about THEMSELVES - why they think they can't succeed):
   - What limitation do they believe about themselves?
   - Example: "I don't have the charisma or network to pull off signal-based selling"

8. **External Belief** (about the WORLD/MARKET - what they think is true about reality):
   - What market condition do they think prevents them?
   - Example: "Enterprise buyers are too sophisticated to respond to systematic outreach"

### The Three Secrets (The Reframes)
9. **Secret 1** — How do you REFRAME the vehicle belief? What's the truth?
   - Example: "Coffee meetings are a tax on growth. Real proof of competence is proof of competence — delivered at the moment intent surfaces"
   - **Secret 1 Framework Name** — (a named framework that proves this):
     - Name: "Pre-Meeting Kill Sheet"
     - What it does: "Demonstrates specific authority before the first conversation"

10. **Secret 2** — How do you REFRAME the internal belief?
    - Example: "You don't need personal charisma — you need systematic proof of competence"
    - **Secret 2 Framework Name:**
      - Name: "The 30-Second Trust Flip"
      - What it does: "Converts skeptics in their first 30 seconds on your page/email"

11. **Secret 3** — How do you REFRAME the external belief?
    - Example: "Enterprise buyers WANT to respond to systematic, competence-first outreach. They're tired of relationship theater."
    - **Secret 3 Framework Name:**
      - Name: "The WSO Cultural Accelerator"
      - What it does: "Maps buying signals to specific cultural behaviors in Gulf markets"

### Stories (One for Origin + One per Secret)
12. **Origin Story** — Your personal transformation story:
    - One specific moment when you realized the old approach was broken
    - What changed?
    - Example: "I spent 3 years doing coffee meetings at Cisco. Closed 2 deals per year. Switched to signal-based outreach. Closed 11 deals in year one."

13. **Story 1** (proves Secret 1) — A specific moment that shows the vehicle belief was wrong:
    - One specific scene
    - Who, what, where, when
    - The belief shift that happened

14. **Story 2** (proves Secret 2) — A specific moment that shows why they CAN do this:
    - One specific scene
    - Proof that they don't need innate talent, they need process

15. **Story 3** (proves Secret 3) — A specific moment that shows the market has changed:
    - One specific scene
    - Proof that buyers ARE responding to signal-based approaches

### Authority & References
16. **Authority sources** you'll reference:
    - Books, researchers, frameworks, data points
    - Example: ["The First 90 Days by Michael Watkins", "Harvard Business Review on Buying Intent", "Gong research on deal velocity"]

### The Offer
17. **What are you selling at the end?**
    - Free: consultation, audit, assessment, checklist, playbook
    - Paid: course, program, consulting engagement, workshop
    - Example: "Free Signal Audit (discover your company's buying intent blind spots) + invitation to the SalesMfast Signal Engine beta"

### Speaker Details
18. **Your credibility (keep it tight):**
    - Name, title, company
    - Key credential (one statistic or fact that proves authority)
    - Example: "Mamoun Alamouri, CEO SMOrchestra.ai | Built signal-based engines for 40+ B2B companies in MENA | 11 deals/year from 2 via cold outreach"

### Campaign Timeline
19. **How many days before the event should the campaign run?**
    - When do posts drop? (5 days before, 3 days before, etc.)
    - Email sends? (3 days, 1 day, day-of, post-event)

### Language
20. **Language preference:**
    - English, Arabic, or bilingual?

## PHASE 1: STORIES

I'll read `references/story-architecture.md` to understand the Epiphany Bridge framework.

Then I'll take your raw story inputs and structure them using:
- **Epiphany Bridge format:** Setup → The Fall/Conflict → Dark Night → The Epiphany → The New Belief
- **"One Specific Moment" principle** — every story anchors on a single scene, not a general narrative
- **Full vs. Compressed versions:**
  - Full: for VSL, YouTube, long-form (3-5 minutes)
  - Compressed: for the deck (90-120 seconds, full emotional arc)

**Output:** `[event-name]-stories.md` with all four stories fully dramatized.

## PHASE 3: SOCIAL POSTS (5 Posts)

I'll read `references/social-templates.md` for post structures.

**5 LinkedIn posts:**
1. **Big Promise Post** (3-5 days before)
   - Opens with origin story hook
   - Authority stack (credentials)
   - CTA: register link

2. **Secret 1 Post** (2-3 days before)
   - Myth-busting angle (vehicle belief broken)
   - Contrarian stat or insight
   - CTA: register

3. **Secret 2 Post** (1-2 days before)
   - Research/science angle
   - Proof that they CAN do it
   - Urgency: "tomorrow I reveal..."
   - CTA: register

4. **Secret 3 Post** (day before or day of)
   - Contrarian thesis (external belief reframed)
   - Last-call urgency
   - CTA: register

5. **Post-Event FOMO Post** (same day, after event)
   - Room energy recap
   - Key reveals (teased, not fully given)
   - "DM me SYSTEM" CTA (mystery mechanic)

Each post includes a comment template to post immediately after (with registration link in the comment, not the body).

**Output:** All 5 posts in `[event-name]-campaign-assets.md`

## PHASE 4: EMAIL SEQUENCE (3 Emails)

I'll read `references/email-templates.md` for email structures.

**3 emails:**
1. **Registration Push** (send 3-5 days before)
   - Subject line A/B test options
   - Story-led hook
   - {{first_name}} personalization
   - Registration link + deadline

2. **Urgency / Last Call** (send day before)
   - Subject line A/B test
   - Stat-led or urgency-led
   - What they'll learn (without spoiling)
   - "Spots filling fast" urgency
   - Registration link

3. **Post-Webinar** (send same day or next day)
   - Recap key moments
   - Teased reveals (not full frameworks)
   - Offer for next step
   - FOMO for recording access
   - CTA to take action

**Output:** All 3 emails in `[event-name]-campaign-assets.md`
