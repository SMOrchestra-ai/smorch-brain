# Content Systems

Systematic content production framework for founders to build authority and audience without constant hustle.

For detailed framework breakdowns (Content GPS, 7 Systems, Hook Points, Content Waterfall, Content-to-Paid Pipeline, Build-in-Public), see frameworks.md.
For tech stack, quick-start checklists, production timelines, and monthly scorecard template, see templates.md.

## Core Principle

**Content is your moat.** In MENA markets where relationships drive business, consistent content moves you from "founder I know" to "founder I follow."

The system: 1 core idea -> 7 formats -> 30 pieces/month -> $30K+ MRR

## The 5 Frameworks

### 1. Matt Gray — Content GPS
Map your content direction: North Star Idea -> 4 Content Pillars -> Weekly Themes -> 12-Month Calendar.
Prevents random, directionless posting. See frameworks.md for the full 3-Tier model and GPS Map Template.

### 2. Matt Gray — 7 Core Systems + 30-in-30
Seven content types, each with specific repurposing paths:

| System | What | Repurpose Into |
|--------|------|---------------|
| Evergreen Piece | 2,000-3,000 word essay | 10 posts + email sequence + video + threads |
| Weekly Insights | 1-2 paragraph atomic idea | 1 email + 1 post + 1 repost |
| Customer Stories | Before/Problem/Intervention/After | Post + email + case study + testimonial |
| Educational | Teach one skill end-to-end | Video + blog + email sequence + carousel |
| Contrarian Takes | Belief-breaking statements | Post + email subject + podcast clip |
| Pattern Recognition | Patterns from 10+ customers | Post + email + blog + podcast |
| Community Engagement | Questions for audience | Post + email + Discord + data compilation |

**30-in-30:** Create 30 pieces in 30 days to build early momentum. See frameworks.md for weekly breakdown.

### 3. Brendan Kane — Hook Points + Content Waterfall
5 hook formulas to apply to every piece:
1. **Contrarian Take** — "Everything founders know about X is wrong"
2. **Data Disruption** — "73% of MENA founders fail because..."
3. **Behind-the-Curtain** — "Here's what happens inside a closed call"
4. **Challenge/Dare** — "I dare you to do X for 72 hours"
5. **Story Hook** — "Last week a Dubai founder told me..."

**Content Waterfall:** 1 core idea -> 7 formats (essay, video, email sequence, carousel, thread, short-form, podcast) -> 30+ total pieces. See frameworks.md for full production workflow.

### 4. Alex Hormozi — Content-to-Paid Pipeline (12 Months)

| Phase | Months | Focus | Revenue Target |
|-------|--------|-------|---------------|
| Audience Building | 1-3 | Daily content, 5K followers | $0 (seed) |
| Lead Generation | 4-6 | Lead magnets, 1K emails | $0 (list building) |
| Low-Ticket Product | 7-9 | $97-197 product launch | $1-2K MRR |
| High-Ticket + Continuity | 10-12 | $2-5K product + membership | $5-10K MRR |

See frameworks.md for detailed monthly breakdown.

### 5. Tibo — Build-in-Public Strategy

4 layers of public building:
- **Progress Updates** (weekly) — Numbers, wins, failures
- **Decision-Making** (real-time) — Ask audience for input
- **Teaching Through Doing** (async) — Document process as you work
- **Vulnerability** (strategic) — Share struggles and pivots

**MENA-Specific:** WhatsApp > Blog for updates. Bilingual (create distinct, don't translate). Community-first metric = "people who know your voice."

## When To Use Which Framework

| Situation | Framework |
|-----------|-----------|
| Starting from zero, need direction | Content GPS (Framework 1) |
| Need to produce volume fast | 30-in-30 (Framework 2) |
| Posts not getting engagement | Hook Points (Framework 3) |
| Have audience, need revenue | Content-to-Paid Pipeline (Framework 4) |
| Building trust in MENA market | Build-in-Public (Framework 5) |
| Weekly content planning | GPS themes + 7 Systems rotation |
| Monthly optimization | Monthly Scorecard (see templates.md) |
