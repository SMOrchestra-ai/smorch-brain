# Content Systems — Detailed Frameworks

## Framework 1: Matt Gray — Content GPS

### The Problem: Content Without Direction

Most founders create random content (sporadic LinkedIn posts). This burns out fast and builds no moat.

Content GPS gives you a map: Clear direction + waypoints + destination.

### The 3-Tier Content GPS Model

**TIER 1: Your North Star Idea**
- One core belief you want to spread
- Example: "MENA founders don't need Silicon Valley GTM; they need founder-led sales"
- Example: "The consultant's playbook is killing your unit economics"
- Example: "Language is your competitive advantage, not liability"

This is your North Star. All content points toward this belief.

**TIER 2: Your Content Pillars (3-4 categories)**

Each pillar is a subcategory that supports your North Star.

Example North Star: "MENA GTM is fundamentally different from US GTM"

Pillar 1: Founder Psychology (Founder behavior, mentality, decision-making)
Pillar 2: Market Dynamics (Gulf markets, relationship selling, pricing psychology)
Pillar 3: Execution Framework (3-Day Launch, founder-led outbound, community building)
Pillar 4: Common Mistakes (Hiring too fast, copying US SaaS, losing to relationships)

**TIER 3: Content Themes (Weekly topics)**

Each week, you pick one theme from your pillars.

Example weekly themes:
- Week 1: "Why hiring a VP of Sales kills MENA startups" (Pillar 1 + Pillar 4)
- Week 2: "The real reason your cold email gets no response in Gulf markets" (Pillar 2)
- Week 3: "How to run a founder-led sales campaign in 72 hours" (Pillar 3)
- Week 4: "The pricing mistake that costs you 50% of revenue" (Pillar 2 + Pillar 4)

### Content GPS Map Template

```
NORTH STAR IDEA:
[One core belief that drives all your content]

PILLAR 1: [Category + 2-3 sentence description]
PILLAR 2: [Category + 2-3 sentence description]
PILLAR 3: [Category + 2-3 sentence description]
PILLAR 4: [Category + 2-3 sentence description]

12-MONTH THEME CALENDAR:
Q1: [4 weekly themes that build on each other]
Q2: [4 weekly themes]
Q3: [4 weekly themes]
Q4: [4 weekly themes]
```

## Framework 2: Matt Gray — 7 Core Systems + 30-in-30

### System 1: The Evergreen Content Piece

One long-form essay that never expires and serves as your content hub.

Examples:
- "The MENA GTM Playbook: Everything I've Learned from 50 Founder Exits"
- "The Founder-Led Sales Handbook: How to Get 10 Customers in 30 Days"

**How to write:** 2,000-3,000 words. Actionable. Specific to MENA. Updated every 3 months.

**Repurposing:** Essay -> 10 LinkedIn posts -> 1 email sequence -> 1 YouTube video -> 3 Twitter threads

### System 2: Weekly Insights (The Atomic Idea)

One small, specific insight published weekly. 1-2 paragraph post with 1 data point or story.
Repurpose into: 1 email, 1 LinkedIn post, 1 repost (next week)

### System 3: Customer Stories (Proof)

**Format:** Before -> Problem -> Intervention -> After (with numbers) -> Lesson
**Repurpose:** 1 LinkedIn post, 1 email, 1 case study page, 1 testimonial, 1 social proof graphic
**MENA Adaptation:** Use founder names + photos (relationship trust > anonymity)

### System 4: Educational Content (Teaching)

Teach a skill your audience needs. Format: Video, guide, or email sequence.
**Repurpose:** 1 YouTube video, 1 blog post, 1 email sequence (5 emails), 1 LinkedIn carousel, 1 checklist

### System 5: Contrarian Takes (Hook Points)

Belief-breaking statements that start conversations.
Format: Provocative statement + 2-3 supporting points + invitation for debate.
**Repurpose:** 1 LinkedIn post, 1 email subject line, 1 podcast clip, 1 community discussion starter

### System 6: Pattern Recognition (Deep Insights)

Share patterns you've noticed across 10+ customers.
Format: Data + pattern naming + actionable insight.
**Repurpose:** 1 LinkedIn post, 1 email, 1 blog post (expanded), 1 podcast episode

### System 7: Community Engagement (Feedback Loop)

Ask questions that get your audience thinking. Genuine question + context + invitation for response.
**Repurpose:** 1 LinkedIn post, 1 email, 1 Discord/group chat discussion, Data compilation

### 30-in-30: Batch Creation System

**Week 1: Foundation (5 pieces)** — Evergreen essay (draft) + 4 micro-videos
**Week 2: Leverage (7 pieces)** — Publish essay + 10 LinkedIn posts + email sequence
**Week 3: Teach (8 pieces)** — YouTube video + email sequence + carousel + thread
**Week 4: Proof + Engagement (10 pieces)** — 2-3 customer stories + 2 case studies + 4 contrarian takes + 2 community questions + 2 patterns

**Total: 30 pieces published across LinkedIn, email, blog, YouTube, Twitter**

## Framework 3: Brendan Kane — Hook Points + Content Waterfall

### The 5 Hook Points

**1. Contrarian Take** — "Everything founders know about GTM is wrong"
**2. Data Disruption** — "73% of MENA founders fail because..."
**3. Behind-the-Curtain** — "Here's what happens inside a closed GTM call"
**4. Challenge/Dare** — "I dare you to do founder-led outreach for 72 hours"
**5. Story Hook** — "Last week a Dubai founder told me..."

**Apply to every piece:** Does your post have ONE hook point? If not, add it.

### The Content Waterfall: One Core Idea -> 7 Formats

**Core Idea:** "The 3-Day GTM Sprint Formula"

| Format | Platform | Details |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Long-Form Essay | Blog, Medium | 2,500 words, SEO-optimized |
| Video Tutorial | YouTube | 15 min, screen recorded |
| Email Sequence | Email | 5 emails, one per day |
| LinkedIn Carousel | LinkedIn | 5 slides |
| Twitter Thread | Twitter/X | 10-15 tweets |
| Short-Form Video | TikTok/Reels | 15-30 sec clips x 3-5 |
| Podcast Episode | Spotify, Apple | 25-40 min solo |

**Waterfall Production Workflow:**
```
Monday: Write 2,000-word essay
Tuesday: Record 15-min video from essay
Wednesday: Create 5-email sequence from essay
Thursday: Design 5-slide LinkedIn carousel
Friday: Write 10-tweet thread
Saturday: Record 5x 15-second Reels clips
Sunday: Record 30-min podcast episode

Result: 1 core idea, 7 formats, 30+ total pieces
```

## Framework 4: Alex Hormozi — Content-to-Paid Pipeline (12 Months)

**Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Audience Building)**
- Publish daily content. Goal: 5,000 followers/email subscribers.
- Focus: Volume + consistency. Monetization: NONE.

**Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Lead Generation)**
- 5x/week. Create lead magnet. Build email list (goal: 1,000+ emails).
- Monetization: Email list (no product yet).

**Phase 3: Months 7-9 (Low-Ticket Product)**
- Launch $97-197 product. Goal: $1-2K MRR.

**Phase 4: Months 10-12 (High-Ticket + Continuity)**
- Launch $2-5K product + $500-1,000/month continuity. Goal: $5-10K MRR.

## Framework 5: Tibo — Build-in-Public Strategy

**Layer 1: Progress Updates (Weekly)** — Share what you're working on, numbers, failures.
**Layer 2: Decision-Making (Real-time)** — Ask audience for input on key decisions.
**Layer 3: Teaching Through Doing (Async)** — Document your process as you do the work.
**Layer 4: Vulnerability (Strategic)** — Share struggles, show failures, admit when wrong.

### MENA-Specific Build-in-Public

- WhatsApp > Blog for real-time updates (daily voice message)
- Bilingual: Arabic for Arabic community, English for international (don't translate—create distinct)
- Community-first metric: "people who know your voice", not followers
