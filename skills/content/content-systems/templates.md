# Content Systems — Templates & Checklists

## Content System Stack (Technology)

**Content Creation:**
- Writing: Google Docs, Notion
- Video: CapCut (editing), Riverside.fm (recording guests)
- Audio: Riverside.fm (podcast), Adobe Audition (editing)

**Scheduling:**
- LinkedIn: LinkedIn creator tools (native)
- Email: Substack or ConvertKit
- Twitter: Buffer or TweetDeck
- Blog: WordPress, Substack, or Ghost

**Repurposing:**
- Repurpose.io (automatically repurposes video to shorts)
- Make.com (automation workflow)
- Copy.ai (AI writing assistant)

**Measurement:**
- LinkedIn Analytics (engagement rate, reach, saves)
- Email Analytics (open rate, click rate)
- YouTube Analytics (watch time, retention)
- Custom spreadsheet (track followers, revenue, email list size monthly)

## Content System Quick-Start

**Week 1: Foundation**
- [ ] Define North Star Idea (1 sentence)
- [ ] Create 4 content pillars
- [ ] Map 12 monthly themes
- [ ] Publish 1 evergreen essay

**Week 2: Production**
- [ ] Write 1 piece of content per day (7 pieces)
- [ ] Repurpose into 3 formats each (21 pieces)
- [ ] Schedule for 1 month ahead

**Week 3: Engagement**
- [ ] Respond to every comment
- [ ] Share audience posts
- [ ] Create 1 community question
- [ ] Measure: Engagement rate, saves, shares

**Week 4: Optimization**
- [ ] Analyze what worked (top 3 posts)
- [ ] Identify your best hook point (Contrarian? Data? Story?)
- [ ] Double down on winning format next month

## Content Production Realistic Timeline

| Available Hours | Weekly Output | Monthly Output |
|-----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 2-3 hours | 1 post + email | 4-5 pieces total |
| 5-7 hours | 3-4 posts + email + video | 15-20 pieces total |
| 10+ hours | Daily content across platforms | 30-40 pieces total |

**For solo founders:** 5-7 hours/week = sustainable growth

**Workflow:** 1-2 hour batch creation session (write 3-4 ideas), then 30-min/day repurposing into formats.
