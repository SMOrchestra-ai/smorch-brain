# Company Profile

## Venture
- Name: SalesMfast Signal Engine
- One-liner: B2B outbound intelligence platform that detects buying signals and sequences multi-channel outreach before competitors react
- Entity: SMOrchestra.ai (SalesMfast product line)
- Project type: tech

## Problem
- Statement: B2B sales teams in the Gulf detect buying intent too late. By the time reps notice a competitor hired SDRs, changed their tech stack, or received funding, the deal is already in someone else's pipeline. The average detection delay is 3-4 weeks, during which competitors who detect faster close 60% of competitive deals
- Who experiences it: VP Sales and Revenue leaders at B2B SaaS companies selling into UAE, Saudi Arabia, and Qatar enterprise markets
- Current workarounds: Manual LinkedIn scanning (2-3 hours/week per rep), personal network pings, CRM notes from outdated meetings, occasional Google Alerts
- Cost of inaction: ~$50K/quarter in missed pipeline per 40-person company, based on 3 deals/month lost to late detection × $16K average deal value

## Market
- Geography: UAE (primary), Saudi Arabia, Qatar (secondary)
- Industry vertical: B2B SaaS companies selling into Gulf enterprise (banking, telecom, government, real estate)
- Market size indicator: ~2,400 B2B SaaS companies with 20-100 employees operating in UAE+Saudi+Qatar (source: DIFC/ADGM registries + LinkedIn Sales Nav search)
- Growth signals: 47% YoY increase in B2B SaaS registrations in UAE (2024-2025), Saudi Vision 2030 digital transformation mandates, growing SDR hiring across Gulf tech companies

## Product/Service
- Category: Outbound intelligence platform
- Core deliverable: Automated signal detection + multi-channel outreach sequencing (email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp)
- Pricing: $1,500/month (Growth), $3,500/month (Scale), $7,000/month (Enterprise — includes managed service)
- Timeline: 2-week onboarding, signal detection live by day 3, first outreach sequences by week 2
