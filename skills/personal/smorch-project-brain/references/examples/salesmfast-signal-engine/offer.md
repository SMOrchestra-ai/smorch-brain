## Guarantee / Risk Reversal
- Type: Performance guarantee (hybrid)
- Statement: "10 qualified meetings from signal-triggered outreach in the first 90 days, or we continue working at no charge until you hit the target"
- Why this guarantee works: (1) Demonstrates confidence in the system, (2) "Qualified meeting" is objectively measurable (meeting held, not just booked), (3) Time-bound (90 days) prevents indefinite free work, (4) "Continue at no charge" is less risky than money-back for a service business
- Why not money-back: Service-based with meaningful delivery cost. Refund would create moral hazard (client could get 90 days of signal infrastructure built for free)
