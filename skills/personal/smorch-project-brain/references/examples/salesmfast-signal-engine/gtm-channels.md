## Channel Priority Stack

### Primary: LinkedIn (Cold Discovery)
- Approach: Sales Navigator search → Signal-triggered connection request with personalized note → Content engagement → Outreach message
- Volume: 50-100 new connections/week, 10-15 conversations/week
- Content: Authority education posts 3x/week, signal-based commentary on prospect activity
- Tool: HeyReach for sequencing, LinkedIn Sales Navigator for targeting

### Primary: WhatsApp (Warm Conversion)
- Approach: After LinkedIn connection accepted OR event meeting → WhatsApp conversation → Value delivery → Meeting booking
- Volume: 20-30 warm conversations/month
- Tone: Conversational Gulf Arabic for MENA, English for international. Greeting → context → value → ask
- Tool: GHL (SalesMfast CRM) for WhatsApp Business API

### Secondary: Email (Cold Outreach + Nurture)
- Approach: Signal-triggered cold sequences (3-email, 5-day spacing). Warm nurture for prospects not ready to meet
- Volume: 200-400 cold emails/week, reply target 6%+
- Tool: Instantly.ai for cold, GHL for warm/nurture

### Tertiary: Events (High-Trust Pipeline)
- Events: GITEX (October), LEAP (March), SaaS-specific meetups in Dubai, industry dinners
- Approach: Pre-event signal research on attendees → Targeted meeting requests → On-site demos → WhatsApp follow-up within 24 hours
- Volume: 2-4 events/year, 20-50 meetings per event
